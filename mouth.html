Title: Self-Portrait with Cropped Hair

Artist: Frida Kahlo (1907-1954)

Year: 1940

Medium: Oil on canvas

Description: "Self-Portrait with Cropped Hair" presents Frida Kahlo in a bold and defiant manner, depicting her with short hair, attired in a man's suit, and clutching a pair of scissors. Stray locks of hair are scattered on the ground around her, adding to the rebellious atmosphere of the painting. Through this provocative self-portrait, Kahlo makes a powerful statement about her defiance and independence, challenging traditional gender roles and societal expectations. It serves as a testament to her willingness to assert her identity on her own terms, embodying her unyielding spirit and determination to transcend boundaries.
