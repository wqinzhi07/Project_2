Title: Self-Portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird

Artist: Frida Kahlo (1907-1954)

Year: 1940

Medium: Oil on canvas

Description: "Self-Portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird" is an iconic representation of Frida Kahlo, featuring her adorned with a thorn necklace and a hovering hummingbird near her neck. The thorns serve as symbols of the pain and suffering she endured throughout her tumultuous life, while the hummingbird embodies vitality and resilience in the face of adversity. Through this self-portrait, Kahlo delves into the exploration of her physical and emotional scars, offering viewers a glimpse into her inner world and struggles. Additionally, the painting demonstrates Kahlo's profound connection to Mexican folk art and symbolism, showcasing her cultural roots and artistic vision.
