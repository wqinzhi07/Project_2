"Self-Portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird" (1940)

This iconic self-portrait depicts Kahlo wearing a thorn necklace with a hummingbird hovering near her neck. The thorns symbolize the pain and suffering she endured throughout her life, while the hummingbird represents vitality and resilience. The painting reflects Kahlo's exploration of her physical and emotional scars, as well as her connection to Mexican folk art and symbolism.
