Title: Self-Portrait with Thorn Necklace and Hummingbird

Artist: Frida Kahlo (1907-1954)

Year: 1932

Medium: Oil on canvas

Description: "My Birth" is a surreal painting that portrays Frida Kahlo's birth, featuring her adult head emerging from her mother's birth canal. The composition is enriched with symbolic elements, such as medical instruments, indigenous imagery, and pre-Columbian idols, reflecting Kahlo's profound fascination with birth, life, and death. Through this artwork, Kahlo delves into themes of fertility, femininity, and mortality, inviting viewers to contemplate the intricacies of existence and the cycle of life.
