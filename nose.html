"My Birth" (1932)

This surreal painting depicts Kahlo's birth, with her adult head emerging from her mother's birth canal. The scene is surrounded by various symbolic elements, including medical instruments, indigenous imagery, and pre-Columbian idols. The painting reflects Kahlo's fascination with birth, life, and death, as well as her exploration of fertility, femininity, and mortality.
